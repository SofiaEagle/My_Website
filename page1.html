Italian Inventions

Italy is known for it's creation of pizza. But did you know
that the ice cream cone and thermometer are also italian inventions?
What about batteries, the piano, the radio, the espresso machine,
eyeglasses, banks, newspapers, jeans, the telephone, the barometer,
the typewriter, the microchip, and the parachute?
These are also italian inventions!













Famous foods in Italy

Pizza and pasta, are arguably the most famous foods in Italy.
But some other tasty foods include Lasagna, Ragu, Cacio e Pepe,
and Arancini. There are also some must-try deserts! Famous deserts
include Tiramisu, Gelato, Cannoli, Panna Cotta, Orange Polenta Cake,
and the Cassata cake.












Popular places in Italy

The most popular place in Italy is Rome. Rome is the capital
city and is rich in history and culture. Rome is famous for
iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon.
Another popular city is Venice. What's so striking about Venice
is that it is built on water!
